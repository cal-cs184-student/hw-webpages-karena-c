What You Have Accomplished

We looked into the tutorials and resources given in our feedback on our proposal. There were several that we found incredibly helpful for identifying how to get started. We followed the tutorial given in Implementing Keyframe Animation (https://veeenu.github.io/blog/implementing-keyframe-animation/) by Andrea Venuta to learn how to achieve the implementation of keyframe animations. We also looked into the blog post Everything You Need to Know About FBX Files- A Comprehensive Guide by Vection Technologies (https://vection-technologies.com/blog/Everything-You-Need-to-Know-About-FBX-Files-A-Comprehensive-Guide/) to understand what FBX files are and how we could use them in our project.
Next, we looked into the papers shared with us to understand and develop implementation strategies. 4D Humans by Shubham Goel, Georgios Pavlakos, Jathushan Rajasegaran, Angjoo Kanazawa, and Jitendra Malik (https://shubham-goel.github.io/4dhumans/) had a very interesting approach that complemented our project a lot as it explored how to track and reconstruct humans in videos. We thought it would be really interesting to understand how they did this to track the dancers in our video clip that we wanted to track, so we delved deeper into understanding their code linked in their GitHub at https://github.com/shubham-goel/4D-Humans.
